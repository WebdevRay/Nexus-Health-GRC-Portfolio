## 3. Regulatory & Legal Landscape
As a Third-Party Administrator of sensitive health data, Nexus Health AI is subject to:
* **HIPAA:** Security and Privacy Rules for handling PHI.
* **CCPA:** California Consumer Privacy Act (based on user location).
* **Contractual Obligations:** Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with hospital systems.

## 4. Key Stakeholders
* **Internal:** Executive Leadership, DevOps Engineers, Data Scientists.
* **External:** Hospital Systems, Patient Advocacy Groups, Insurance Payers.

## 5. Risk Appetite Statement
Nexus Health AI has a **Low Risk Appetite** regarding Service Availability and Data Privacy. While the firm encourages rapid innovation in AI modeling, all changes to the production environment must undergo rigorous "Safety Net" (Peer Review) controls to prevent system downtime.
