Scope Statement: Nexus Health AI Information Security Program
1. Purpose: This document defines the boundaries of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) for Nexus Health AI to ensure the availability of AI diagnostic tools and the protection of patient data.
2. Organizational Scope: This program covers all internal business operations, including the 50-person remote workforce, the AWS cloud production environment, and the proprietary "Nexus-Core" AI engine.
3. Asset Scope: > * Logical: All PHI/PII stored within AWS RDS instances, AI model weights, and the CI/CD pipeline in GitHub.
* Physical: Corporate-issued MacBooks and home-office network security standards (Remote-First).
4. Regulatory Scope: Compliance is maintained against HIPAA Security/Privacy Rules and SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria (Availability, Security, and Confidentiality).
5. Primary Objective: To maintain 99.9% uptime for the AI diagnostic API and ensure zero unauthorized disclosure of patient medical records.
