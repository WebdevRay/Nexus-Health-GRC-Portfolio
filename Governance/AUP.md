# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP): Nexus Health AI

## 1. Overview
The purpose of this policy is to outline the acceptable use of corporate technology assets at Nexus Health AI. These rules are in place to protect the availability of our AI services and the privacy of patient data.

## 2. Applicability
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party agents operating on behalf of Nexus Health AI.

## 3. Remote Work & Connectivity (The "Assessor" Rule)
* **Secure Connections:** Employees must utilize the company-approved VPN for all access to internal systems and AWS environments.
* **Public Wi-Fi:** The use of unsecured public Wi-Fi for accessing PHI or production systems is strictly prohibited. 
* **Physical Security:** MacBooks must never be left unattended in public spaces. Privacy screens are required when working in high-traffic areas.

## 4. System Integrity & Availability
* Employees shall not attempt to bypass security controls, including MFA or endpoint protection.
* Unauthorized installation of software that could interfere with system stability is prohibited.

## 5. Reporting
Any suspected security incident or loss of equipment must be reported to the Security Team within **1 hour** of discovery.

## 6. Policy Compliance
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
